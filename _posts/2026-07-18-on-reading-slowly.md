title: On reading slowly
category: Reading note
excerpt: Reading slowly is not a refusal of the world’s speed. It is a way of noticing what speed leaves behind.
---

Some books do not open at once. They need weather, silence, and the chance to be set down for a while.

To read slowly is to let a book continue speaking when it is closed. A sentence becomes useful not because it is memorable, but because it begins to rearrange the questions you ask.
